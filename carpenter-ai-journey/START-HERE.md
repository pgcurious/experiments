## Who Is This For?

### This book is FOR you if:
- You're skilled at a trade/craft but distant from technology
- You use a basic phone or struggle with smartphones
- You want to grow your business but dread "learning tech"
- You feel "too old" or "not tech-savvy enough"
- You worry AI will replace your craft

### This book might NOT be for you if:
- You're already comfortable with apps, AI tools, automation
- You're looking for advanced AI implementation strategies
- You want to learn programming or technical AI
- You don't have a craft/trade/skill you want to protect and grow
